### Cybersecurity Analyst

Some job offers and what they require:

- Cyber Security Analyst: Have a Cyber Security qualification such as PCIRM or CISMP
- Senior Cyber Response Analyst: GIAC certification preferable
- Critical Incident Response Center (CIRC) Analyst: CISSP, GCIA, GCIH, GREM or applicable experience in the Information Security field

### Engineer/Architect

Some job offers and what they require:

- Cybersecurity Systems Engineer: A cyber security certification, such as CISSP.
- Cyber Security Architect: Bachelor/Master of Science degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Telecommunications or another related technical degree (Relevant certs are an advantage, e.g. TOGAF, ITIL, PMP, ISO27k LA/LI, (ISC)2 CISSP, ISACA CISM/CISA/CRISC, SANS GIAC).
